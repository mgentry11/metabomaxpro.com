What is High Intensity Training?

⚡

Train to Failure Safely

HIT focuses on taking each exercise to momentary muscle failure - the point where you cannot complete another rep with proper form. Slow tempos make this safer than ever.

🕒

Time Under Tension

Extended time under load is the key stimulus for muscle growth. The 30-20-40 tempo ensures your muscles work hard throughout the entire 90-second set.

🎯

One Set, Maximum Effect

When performed correctly, a single set to failure per exercise provides the stimulus needed for growth. Brief, intense workouts that respect your time.

How It Works

1

Choose Your Profile and Workout

Select P1 or P2 profile, then choose Workout A or B. The app remembers your last weights for each exercise.

2

Tap to Start an Exercise

The timer begins with a prep countdown (10 sec) followed by positioning time (5 sec) to get into the eccentric position.

3

Follow Voice Coaching

The app guides you through each phase with voice commands and visual timer. Focus on the muscle - the app handles timing.

  • Eccentric (30s): Slow, controlled lowering
  • Concentric (20s): Powerful lifting phase
  • Final Eccentric (40s): Extended negative to failure
4

Log Your Set

After the set, enter the weight used and mark whether you reached muscle failure. Tap "Log Set" to save to history.

5

Rest and Continue

Rest timer counts down (configurable 30-180 seconds). Voice announces next exercise as you prepare to continue.
