HIT Coach Pro

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

HIT Coach Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web app.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Workout Data: Exercise names, weights used, repetitions, and timestamps
  • Settings: Your preferences for voice coaching, phase timings, and app configuration
  • Progress History: Your workout logs and personal records

This data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage or iOS app storage. We do not transmit this data to any external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Health data beyond workout logs you choose to enter
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Payment information (the app is free to use)

How Your Data Is Used

Your workout data is used solely to:

  • Display your workout history and progress
  • Track personal records
  • Maintain your app settings between sessions

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your workout data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

  • iOS App: Data is stored in the app's sandboxed storage
  • Web App (PWA): Data is stored in browser localStorage

Third-Party Services

HIT Coach Pro does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Camera and Microphone

  • Camera: The iOS app requests camera access only for the QR code scanner feature, used to import workout data. Camera data is processed locally and never transmitted.
  • Microphone: The app does not use your microphone. Voice coaching is output-only using text-to-speech.

Children's Privacy

HIT Coach Pro is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • iOS App: Delete the app from your device
  • Web App: Clear your browser's localStorage or use the app's settings to reset data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
