The Protocol Principles

One Set to Failure

Each exercise is performed for a single set taken to complete muscular failure. This maximizes the growth stimulus while minimizing fatigue and recovery demands.

Controlled Tempo

30-second eccentric (lowering), 20-second concentric (lifting), 40-second final eccentric. This extended time under tension is the key to HIT's effectiveness.

Alternating Routines

Alternate between Workout A and B, 2-3 times per week. This provides complete recovery while hitting all major muscle groups twice weekly.

Progressive Resistance

Increase weight by 5-10% when you can complete the full set (90 seconds total time under tension) with good form. Steady, sustainable progress.
